# Business Case

## Why this project mattered
Implementing Loop Returns was a cross-functional transformation that touched:
- Direct-to-consumer operations
- Customer Service
- Warehouse receiving
- Product Services / inspection workflows
- Finance / accounting controls
- Systems: Shopify, Loop, Celigo, NetSuite

This was not “just a tool launch.” It was a redesign of how returns are initiated, tracked, received, refunded, and recorded financially.

## Core business drivers
1. **Visibility**
   - Know when returns are initiated and in transit
   - Reduce “Where is my refund?” escalations and investigation time

2. **Efficiency**
   - Replace paper forms + manual routing with a standard digital portal
   - Reduce the number of human steps required to complete a return

3. **Accuracy & control**
   - Reduce record creation errors (RA, item receipt, credit memo)
   - Ensure refund amounts align across systems (taxes, discounts, customer segments)

4. **Customer experience**
   - Faster, clearer returns process
   - More predictable refund timelines
