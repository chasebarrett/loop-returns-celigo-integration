# Outcomes

This implementation replaced a highly manual, opaque returns process with a structured and partially automated workflow spanning Shopify, Loop, Celigo, and NetSuite.

---

## Operational improvements

- Improved visibility across the entire return lifecycle, from customer initiation through refund and financial recording
- Reduced manual workload across Customer Service, Product Services, and Finance
- Lowered error rates associated with manually created Return Authorizations, Item Receipts, and Credit Memos
- Preserved operational control at the warehouse level while still accelerating refund timelines
- Delivered faster, more accurate refunds to customers
- Maintained clean, auditable financial records in NetSuite

---

## Organizational and cost impact

By centralizing return initiation in Loop and restructuring how returns were received, inspected, and processed, the overall operational load on **Product Services** was significantly reduced.

The reduction in manual return handling was material enough that during **Spring seasonal turnover**, SCARPA was able to **reduce the number of seats in the Product Services department**, resulting in additional cost savings beyond the efficiency gains of the tooling itself.

This outcome demonstrated that improvements in systems and workflows can directly influence staffing requirements—not just process speed or accuracy.
